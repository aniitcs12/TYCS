Blabber All Day is a multi-cuisine restaurant in Mumbai, India with locations in Borivali East and Juhu: Serves a variety of cuisines, including Chinese, North Indian, Italian, and more
Ambiance: Known for its cozy, modern, and elegant ambiance
Popular dishes: Some popular dishes include tiramisu, cheesecake, salmon sushi, basil fish, dumplings, and Kerala style chicken
